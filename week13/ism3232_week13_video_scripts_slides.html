🗣 🗣 Business rules — good vs bad

The most commonly wrong field. Bad: 'validate data'. That is not a business rule. Bad: 'process requests'. That is not a rule — it is a description. Good: 'Leave requests exceeding 10 days require HR director approval.' Good: 'Purchase requests over $1,000 are automatically flagged for manager review.' Good means: there is a threshold, a condition, and a consequence. If you cannot express it as a Python conditional, it is not specific enough.
